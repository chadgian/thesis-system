Welcome to our Document Similarity and Retrieval tool!
We offer three powerful options to meet your document analysis needs

Option 1: "Doc vs. Multi"

This option allows you to compare one specific document against a set of multiple documents. Simply input your primary document, and our system will efficiently analyze its similarities and differences with each document in the subsequent input. It's a straightforward way to assess how a single document aligns with a batch of others.

Doc vs. Multi
Option 2: "Multi-File Matrix"

With this feature, you can input multiple files, and our system will perform a comprehensive comparison. Each file is compared against every other file, creating a matrix-like table that highlights the relationships and similarities between all the files. It's an efficient way to analyze the pairwise document similarities within a set of multiple files, offering a holistic view of their relationships.

Multi-File Matrix
Option 3: "Document Retrieval"

"Document Retrieval" simplifies the search process within a designated directory. By selecting a directory, the system thoroughly scans and explores the content of each document within. This feature streamlines the process of locating specific information or patterns within a directory, providing a convenient and effective means of retrieving relevant documents based on their content.
